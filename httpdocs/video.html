SAM JINKS: BODY IN TIME

Sam Jinks. Body in Time is the first retrospective exhibition of one of Australia's most talented young artists. The works fit within an aesthetic of ‘real sculpture': silicone-based objects that immaculately replicate human form and features.

MUSEUM OF NOTHING SPECIAL:
CHRISTINE WILLCOCKS

Christine Willcocks examines modes of display and how the museum context validates the cultural worth of objects. Willcocks subverts this construct by presenting factual information, small follies and intentional falsities.

DADANG CHISTANTO: SURVIVOR

Survivor is a major art project by contemporary Indonesian-Australian artist Dadang Christanto. Initially developed in 2008 as a live protest against an environmental disaster that occurred in East Java which saw hot volcanic mud wipe out 11 villages and destroy many lives.
